About Me

I am Andy Tan Boon Ping, currently pursuing a degree of Bachelor of Science With Honours (Business Mathematics) [B.Sc. (Hons) Business Mathematics] in Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM). I have learned programming and web development skills alongside with my primary study in university. Below is some brief summary of my past experiences:
  • First learnt programming in the year 2017. Get to know about web development in the lower half of the year.
  • Dive deeper into web development starting from the year 2018. During the period, I developed my first project with Vue, which is a game for primary school kids in UUM Math Camp 2018 event.
  • Fast forward to the year 2019, I developed my second project with UUM Math Camp 2019, which the project required me to make a board game in conjunction with other games in the event. The board game is created by using React and TypeScript
  • In the same year, I started my hobby project, Shuffler, requested by my friend. Shuffler is an app that randomizes user existing YouTube playlist and play it. It starts simple with React, then refactored into TypeScript in July for better maintainability.
  • After Shuffler, I get my hands on some other projects by using other tech/languages as well, such as notes-gallery (PHP), Newster (Go & Vue) and so on. More projects are shown in my github repository.
During my free time, I am constantly keeping myself updated with the latest technology in front-end world as well. Besides front-end app, I started to dive into the backend world for a better understanding on the overall working mechanism of the web.
